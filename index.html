How does it work?

Project Pi uses liquid staking to lower the PulseChain minimum staking requirement to launch a node and become a validator. Reward cycles occur every 15 days and yield is generatedfor liquid stakers and node operators.

LIQUID STAKING

Swap PLS for stPLS to earn staking rewards via liquid staking. No hardware needed.
